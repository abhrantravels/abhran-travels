Trip Overview

Ultra-Modern Skylines & Ancient Dunes

Dubai is the city of superlatives — the world's tallest building, the largest mall, a ski resort inside a desert. Yet just beyond the glittering skyline lies the ancient Rub' al Khali desert with its shifting orange dunes.

🏙 Burj Khalifa At the Top🛒 Dubai Mall & Fountain🐪 Desert Safari🛥 Dubai Creek Dhow Cruise🎿 Ski Dubai🌴 Palm Jumeirah

Day-wise Itinerary

1
Arrival & City Intro

Arrive at Dubai International Airport. Check-in to hotel. Evening: Dubai Fountain show and walk along Dubai Mall waterfront.

2
Dubai Highlights

Morning: Burj Khalifa 'At the Top' — 124th floor views. Afternoon: Dubai Museum and Gold Souk in old Dubai. Evening: Dhow Cruise dinner on Dubai Creek.

3
Desert Safari

Morning at leisure. Afternoon: red dune desert safari — dune bashing, camel riding, sandboarding. Evening: BBQ dinner in Bedouin camp with belly dancing.

4
Modern Dubai

Visit Palm Jumeirah and Atlantis. Aquaventure Waterpark (optional). Afternoon: Jumeirah Beach and the sail-shaped Burj Al Arab drive-by.

5
Ski Dubai & Shopping

Morning: Ski Dubai in Mall of the Emirates — snowboarding in the desert! Afternoon: Dubai Mall and shopping. Evening: Dubai Frame for city panorama.

6
Departure

Morning: last shopping or Global Village (seasonal). Transfer to airport. Farewell to the city of gold!
