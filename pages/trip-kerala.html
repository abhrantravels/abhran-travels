Trip Overview

God's Own Country

Kerala, nestled on the southwest coast of India, is a land of unparalleled natural beauty. From the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey to the emerald tea gardens of Munnar and the pristine beaches of Kovalam, this 6-day journey takes you through Kerala's most iconic landscapes.

🚢 Houseboat Stay🌿 Tea Garden Visit🐘 Elephant Sanctuary🏖 Kovalam Beach🎭 Kathakali Show🛶 Backwater Cruise

Day-wise Itinerary

1
Arrival in Kochi

Welcome to Kerala! Transfer to hotel. Afternoon: Explore Fort Kochi — Chinese fishing nets, Mattancherry Palace. Evening: Kathakali dance performance.

2
Kochi to Munnar

Scenic drive to Munnar through winding mountain roads. Visit Cheeyappara Waterfalls. Check-in to hill resort. Evening walk through tea plantations.

3
Munnar Exploration

Visit Eravikulam National Park. Afternoon: Tea museum and factory tour. Visit Echo Point and Kundala Lake.

4
Alleppey Houseboat

Drive to Alleppey. Board your private houseboat at noon. Cruise through scenic backwaters. Gourmet Kerala meals onboard. Overnight on houseboat.

5
Kovalam Beach

Disembark morning. Drive to Kovalam Beach. Relax on Lighthouse Beach, swimming, water sports. Sunset cocktails. Fresh seafood dinner.

6
Departure Day

Morning Ayurvedic spa (optional). Souvenir shopping. Transfer to Trivandrum Airport. End of a beautiful journey!
