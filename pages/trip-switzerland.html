Trip Overview

Europe's Most Breathtaking Landscapes

Switzerland is the jewel of Europe — a country of impossibly green valleys, mirror-like lakes, chocolate-box villages and the towering Alps. Ride the legendary Glacier Express, cruise Lake Lucerne, and stand atop Jungfraujoch — the Top of Europe.

🚂 Glacier Express Train🏔 Jungfraujoch Summit🛥 Lake Lucerne Cruise🍫 Chocolate Factory⛷ Skiing Options🏙 Zurich & Geneva

Day-wise Itinerary

1
Arrival in Zurich

Arrive at Zurich Airport. Transfer to city hotel. Evening walk along Lake Zurich. Dinner at an old-town restaurant. Welcome to Switzerland!

2
Zurich to Lucerne

Morning: Zurich old town. Afternoon: train to Lucerne — Chapel Bridge, Lion Monument. Evening boat cruise on Lake Lucerne.

3
Jungfraujoch

Full day at Jungfraujoch — Top of Europe at 3,454m. Cogwheel railway up Eiger. Sphinx Observatory, Aletsch Glacier views. Unforgettable!

4
Interlaken

Explore Interlaken between two lakes. Optional: paragliding over the Alps. Afternoon: Harder Kulm viewpoint by funicular. Evening in city.

5
Glacier Express

Board the legendary Glacier Express from Andermatt to Zermatt. 8 hours of breathtaking scenery through 91 tunnels and 291 bridges.

6
Zermatt & Matterhorn

Car-free Zermatt village. Cable car to Klein Matterhorn (3,883m). Epic views of the iconic Matterhorn peak. Swiss fondue dinner.

7
Montreux & Geneva

Drive along Lake Geneva shore. Château de Chillon. Montreux Jazz Walk. Arrive Geneva — United Nations, Jet d'Eau fountain.

8
Departure

Morning: Geneva old town and shopping. Transfer to Geneva Airport. Auf Wiedersehen, Switzerland!
