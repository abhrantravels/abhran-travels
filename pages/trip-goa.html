Trip Overview

Sun, Sand, Seafood & Sunsets

Goa, India's smallest state, packs an enormous punch — Portuguese heritage churches, golden beaches, spicy vindaloo, feni cocktails, and a nightlife scene unlike anywhere else in India. Perfect for a quick, energising getaway.

🏖 Calangute Beach🏛 Portuguese Churches🛥 Sunset Cruise🍤 Seafood Feast🎵 Beach Nightlife🛵 Scooter Ride

Day-wise Itinerary

1
Arrival & North Goa

Arrive at Goa Airport. Check-in to beach resort. Afternoon: Calangute and Baga Beach. Sunset at Anjuna cliff. Seafood dinner at beach shack.

2
Heritage & Water Sports

Morning: Se Cathedral and Basilica of Bom Jesus in Old Goa. Afternoon: water sports at Candolim — jet ski, parasailing, banana boat. Evening: Tito's Street.

3
South Goa

Drive to peaceful South Goa. Palolem Beach — pristine and uncrowded. Visit Cabo de Rama Fort for ocean panoramas. Sunset boat cruise.

4
Departure

Morning: last beach walk and souvenir shopping at Mapusa Market. Transfer to Goa Airport. See you again, Goa!
