Trip Overview

Himalayas, Snow & Thrills

Nestled in the Kullu Valley at 2,050m elevation, Manali is Himachal Pradesh's adventure capital. Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley, ancient temples and the mighty Beas River make it India's most thrilling mountain destination.

❄ Rohtang Pass Snow🧗 Rock Climbing🏂 Skiing & Snowboarding🛕 Hadimba Temple🚵 Mountain Biking🌊 River Rafting

Day-wise Itinerary

1
Arrival in Manali

Arrive at Bhuntar Airport or Manali bus stop. Transfer to hotel. Evening stroll on Mall Road. Hot Maggi and chai at a mountain café.

2
Rohtang Pass

Early morning drive to Rohtang Pass (3,978m). Snow activities — sledging, snowball fights. Spectacular views of glaciers and peaks. Return via Solang Valley.

3
Adventure Day

White-water rafting on River Beas (Grade 3–4). Afternoon: Ziplining and rock climbing at Solang. Evening bonfire.

4
Kullu & Local Culture

Visit the ancient Hadimba Devi Temple in cedar forest. Explore Old Manali. Kullu Shawl factories. Village walk in Naggar. Traditional Himachali dinner.

5
Departure

Morning: final mountain views from Jogini Waterfall trail (short hike). Transfer to Bhuntar Airport or bus stand. End of adventure!
