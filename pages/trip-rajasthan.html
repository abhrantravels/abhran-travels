Trip Overview

Forts, Palaces & Desert Sunsets

Rajasthan is India's most regal state — a land of towering forts, ornate palaces, vibrant markets, and endless sand dunes. This 6-day circuit connects Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer to give you the full royal experience.

🏰 Amber Fort🐪 Desert Camel Safari🌇 Jodhpur Blue City⭐ Jaisalmer Golden Fort🎆 Cultural Show🛍 Handicraft Markets

Day-wise Itinerary

1
Jaipur — The Pink City

Arrive in Jaipur. Visit Amber Fort and Sheesh Mahal. Evening: City Palace and Jantar Mantar observatory. Dinner at heritage haveli.

2
Jaipur Exploration

Morning: Hawa Mahal photo stop. Visit Jal Mahal from lakeside. Afternoon: local bazaars — gems, textiles, handicrafts. Evening folk dance show.

3
Jaipur to Jodhpur

Drive to Jodhpur. Check-in to heritage hotel. Visit Mehrangarh Fort — one of India's most imposing. Explore the Blue City lanes at dusk.

4
Jodhpur to Jaisalmer

Drive through desert landscape to Jaisalmer. Visit the stunning Jaisalmer Fort — still a living fort with residents. Evening: Patwon Ki Haveli.

5
Desert Safari

Morning: Sam Sand Dunes. Camel safari through the Thar Desert. Sunset with folk music and dance. Overnight stay in desert camp under stars.

6
Departure

Morning: Gadsisar Lake and local market. Transfer to Jaisalmer or Jodhpur Airport. End of the royal journey.
