Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Exploration

Abhran Travel was founded in 2016 by a group of passionate travellers who believed that every person deserves to experience the world's wonders — without the stress of planning. What started as a small operation in Jaipur has grown into a trusted travel consultancy serving thousands of adventurers across India.

The name "Abhran" is inspired by the Sanskrit word meaning "to wander through the skies" — reflecting our belief that travel liberates the soul. We combine deep local knowledge with world-class service to deliver journeys that go beyond sightseeing.
